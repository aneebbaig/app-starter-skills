# Code of conduct

## The short version

Be respectful. Assume good faith. Keep discussion focused on the work. No
harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination of any kind.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any
other channel tied to the repo.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and
contributions that do not follow this code, and may block repeat offenders. To
report a problem, contact the maintainer directly or open a private advisory
rather than a public issue.
